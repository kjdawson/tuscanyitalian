About Us

Tuscany Italian Restaurant is a family friendly establishment located in Baytown, Texas. Our philosophy is focused on authentic food, friendly service and fresh ingredients. We are known for our comfortable family-like atmosphere and hospitality. We always strive to provide all our customers with the best possible service every single visit. Our customers in turn have great things to say about us.
